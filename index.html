Tyrannosaurus Rex

It's definitely strange to see the tyrant this low, but here on my list,its at the very bottom

Tyrannosaurus was a north american theropod dinosaur, this 12 tonne, 13 meter beast was the most massive land predator to ever exist, it was discovered in 1902, this coelurosaur lived in the hell creek formation, this formation stretches over portions of Montana, North Dakota, South Dakota, and Wyoming, it lived in Maastrichtian age of the late cretaceous, the very end of the age of the dinosaurs
